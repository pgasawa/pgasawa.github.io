Research

I work on continual learning, studying how we can enable models to learn from streams of experiences over time. Much of my research focuses on sample-efficient learning.

Currently, some areas I work on include:

  • Sample-Efficient Parametric Learning from Natural Language
  • Learned Modules for Adapting Black-Box Models
  • Benchmarks for Continual Learning
